# Skill Test

**Skill Test** is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that turns your CV into personalized skill assessments.  
Upload your resume, extract your key technical and soft skills, and take targeted tests designed to help you prepare for real interview scenarios.

The platform focuses on helping you identify your strengths, improve weak areas, and gain confidence before your next opportunity.

## ✨ Features

- 📄 CV parsing and skill extraction  
- 🧠 AI-generated skill-based tests (MCQ + text)  
- 🎯 Personalized tests based on your background  
- 📊 Performance tracking and insights  
- 🚀 Focused interview preparation
